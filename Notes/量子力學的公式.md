1. Bra-Ket 基本記法（最容易崩的點）

$$ |\psi\rangle,\ \langle\psi|,\ \langle\phi|\psi\rangle $$

$$ \langle n | \hat{x} | m \rangle = \sqrt{\frac{\hbar}{2m\omega}} \left( \sqrt{n+1}\delta_{n+1,m} + \sqrt{n}\delta_{n-1,m} \right) $$

$$ \langle \psi | \hat{A} | \psi \rangle = \int \psi^*(x) \hat{A} \psi(x) \, dx $$


2. 投影算符與完備性關係

$$ \hat{1} = \sum_n |n\rangle\langle n| = \int |\psi\rangle\langle\psi| \, d\psi $$

$$ \hat{P}_i = |i\rangle\langle i| $$

3. 交換子與對易關係

$$ [\hat{x},\hat{p}] = i\hbar $$

$$ [\hat{L}_x, \hat{L}_y] = i\hbar \hat{L}_z $$

4. Pauli 矩陣（最常見的 2×2 矩陣）

$$ \sigma_x = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix},\ 
   \sigma_y = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & -i \\ i & 0 \end{pmatrix},\ 
   \sigma_z = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{pmatrix} $$

$$ \boldsymbol{\sigma} = ( \sigma_x, \sigma_y, \sigma_z ) $$

5. 密度矩陣（含 trace 與純態判斷）

$$ \hat{\rho} = \sum_i p_i |\psi_i\rangle\langle\psi_i| $$

$$ \mathrm{Tr}(\hat{\rho}) = 1,\ \ \mathrm{Tr}(\hat{\rho}^2) = 1 \quad (\text{純態}) $$

$$ \hat{\rho} = \frac{1}{2} \left( \mathbb{I} + \vec{r} \cdot \vec{\sigma} \right) \quad (|\vec{r}| \le 1) $$

6. 時間演化算符與薛丁格方程

$$ i\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t} |\psi(t)\rangle = \hat{H} |\psi(t)\rangle $$

$$ |\psi(t)\rangle = \hat{U}(t,t_0) |\psi(t_0)\rangle = \mathcal{T} \exp\left( -\frac{i}{\hbar} \int_{t_0}^t \hat{H}(t') dt' \right) |\psi(t_0)\rangle $$

7. 角動量與 Clebsch-Gordan 係數（超硬核）

$$ \hat{\mathbf{J}}^2 |j,m\rangle = \hbar^2 j(j+1) |j,m\rangle $$

$$ \hat{J}_z |j,m\rangle = \hbar m |j,m\rangle $$

$$ |j_1 j_2; J M\rangle = \sum_{m_1 m_2} \langle j_1 m_1 j_2 m_2 | J M \rangle |j_1 m_1\rangle |j_2 m_2\rangle $$

8. 張量積與 Bell 態（多粒子系統）

$$ |\uparrow\downarrow\rangle = |\uparrow\rangle \otimes |\downarrow\rangle $$

$$ |\Psi^-\rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \left( |\uparrow\downarrow\rangle - |\downarrow\uparrow\rangle \right) $$

$$ \hat{A} \otimes \hat{B} = \begin{pmatrix} 
A_{11}\hat{B} & A_{12}\hat{B} \\ A_{21}\hat{B} & A_{22}\hat{B} 
\end{pmatrix} $$

9. Dirac 方程與 γ 矩陣（相對論量子）

$$ (i\gamma^\mu \partial_\mu - m)\psi = 0 $$

$$ \{\gamma^\mu, \gamma^\nu\} = 2g^{\mu\nu}\mathbb{I}_4 $$

10. 壓力測試綜合大招（一次全中）

$$
\langle \psi | e^{-i\hat{H}t/\hbar} \hat{x} e^{i\hat{H}t/\hbar} | \psi \rangle 
   = \sum_{n,m} c_n^* c_m e^{i(E_n - E_m)t/\hbar} \langle n | \hat{x} | m \rangle
$$
